Store Policy: Returns

1. Return Window:
Our standard returns policy allows you to return products within 30 days of the delivery date. Items returned after this 30-day window cannot be accepted. Therefore, returning a product after 15 days is fully allowed as it falls within the 30-day window.

2. Condition Requirements for Returns:
- Items must be in their original, unused, and unworn condition.
- All original packaging, tags, labels, and accessories must be intact and returned with the item.
- Shoe products must be returned in their original shoe boxes, which must also be in good condition and placed inside a separate shipping box to prevent damage.
- Items showing signs of wear, washing, alteration, or damage cannot be accepted for returns.

3. How to Initiate a Return:
- Go to our website and log into your account.
- Navigate to the "My Orders" page, locate the order you wish to return, and click on the "Initiate Return" button.
- Select the items you want to return and select the reason for the return.
- Print the prepaid return shipping label provided by the system.
- Pack the items securely, attach the shipping label, and drop off the package at any authorized carrier location.
- Once we receive and inspect the returned items, we will notify you of the status.
