Store Policy: Shipping Information

1. Shipping Timelines:
- Standard Shipping: Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Delivery takes 3 to 5 business days after shipment.
- Express Shipping: Orders are processed within 1 business day. Delivery takes 1 to 2 business days after shipment.
- International Shipping: Delivery times vary between 7 to 14 business days depending on the destination country and customs clearance processes.

2. Shipping Costs:
- Standard Shipping: Free for all orders over $50.00. Otherwise, a flat rate of $4.99 applies.
- Express Shipping: Flat rate of $14.99 for all orders.
- International Shipping: Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

3. Shipping Carriers:
We primarily partner with FedEx, UPS, and DHL to ensure secure and timely deliveries.

4. Order Tracking:
Once your order ships, we will email you a tracking link. You can track your shipment live by clicking the link or entering the tracking ID on the carrier website.
Please contact support if your package does not arrive within the expected timeframe.
